My Story

My name is Kevin Mandeville, but I'm often called Pook for the infamous winter hat I wear. I'm a 25-year old product/web/email designer born and bred in Boston, MA.

I took an Intro to HTML course my freshman year of high school in 2004. I made text move across the screen with the <marquee> tag in my first class. It was love at first pixel. From there, I started pimping out MySpace profiles and taught myself how to code. I've haven't stopped since (coding that is, not MySpace =P).

I've since become immensely passionate about email design. I'm working hard to solve email design problems and make email better at Litmus. I'm currently writing a self-published book titled The Email Design Handbook. I also co-host The Email Design Podcast with Jason Rodriguez. I've also written about and spoken at a few events on the topic of email design which you can see below.

Outside of email, I'm a diehard Boston sports fan and never miss a game. I'm pretty nifty at streetball and was probably a Harlem Globetrotter in a former life. I listen to a lot of pop punk and jazz music. Soylent now accounts for ~70% of my diet—bacon, Baileys, A&W Cream Soda, and peanut butter make up the rest. If you didn't notice, I'm on team Oxford Comma. Cape Cod is my summer vacation spot and I can never get enough skimboarding time each year.

I'm currently undergoing my Total Money Makeover and can't wait to do my debt free scream in 2017. I look forward to traveling the world after that.
